The Future With Sage Business Cloud Enterprise Management and CPiO

Once Enterprise Management technology is in place you can embark on an exciting new future for your organisation. Employees can quickly access reliable data, freeing up the time they would’ve spent generating reports and spreadsheets to gain insights and make effective decisions that support tactical and strategic operations.

Become an Agile Organisation

Sage Business Cloud Enterprise Management makes it easy to try out new ideas, test them, tweak them and repeat. In addition, it provides the foundation for technological innovation – from the integration of the Internet of Things (IoT) to delivering the next step in process automation to the potential of Artificial Intelligence in areas such as customer service.

Plan For the Future With Confidence

Building on trusted business insights, you can explore a raft of tools including forecasting, 'what ifs' and sensitivity planning all designed to help you respond to the unpredictable, and confidently plan for the future.

“Accurate real-time data is the key to accurate forecasting.” -
Brian King, Enterprise Management Product Manager. CPIO

With new and accurate data at your fingertips,
the benefits start to accelerate:
At-a-glance performance
From sales to gross margins to marketing and campaign results, an integrated enterprise system provides immediate, up-to-date KPI tracking.
Ubiquitous access
Mobile access to financial information means you can respond immediately to funding questions from banks, donors or investors.
Management by exception
Automated straight-through invoice processing that only highlights unpaid or queried invoices, and effective supplier management that automatically spots quality issues, means you can focus directly on known problems, leaving business as usual to manage itself.

“In an enterprise system, vast quantities of data are automatically collected – the opportunity is to look at that data in new places, in new ways.” -
Brian King, Enterprise Management Product Manager, CPIO.
