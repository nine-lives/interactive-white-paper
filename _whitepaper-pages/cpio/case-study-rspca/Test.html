The UK’s leading animal welfare charity chose Sage and Platinum Partner CPiO to deliver a flexible, future-proof business management system

Founded nearly 200 years ago, in 1824, the RSPCA is now one of the UK’s largest charities, withan income of £140M and over 1,600 employees.

It has a large network of regional offices, 30 animal homes, hospital and clinics, and a team of uniformed inspectors on the streets. The Organisation investigates over 150,000 complaints of cruelty and neglect every year. The RSPCA’s vision is to live in a world where all animals are respected and treated with compassion.

It works to:
Improve the welfare of pet animals
Improve the lives and reduce the suffering of farm animals
Reduce the use and suffering of animals in experiments

All of its income is raised through voluntary donations, legacies and memberships and fundraising with corporate partners.
