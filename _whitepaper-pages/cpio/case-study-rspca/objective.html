SAGE & CPIO GIVE RSPCA PAWS FOR THOUGHT

Objective: To Deliver a Flexible, Future-proof Business Management System

{% include case-study-rspca-tabs.html active='objective' %}

“We needed a solution that would enable us to take control of the business, and maximise our donations, so that they are focused on animal welfare and not administration costs. We also wanted a solution that had longevity. We’re very aware, sadly, that the need for our services is not going away any time soon.” - Helen Tracey, Chief Financial Officer for RSPCA

RSPCA wanted a plug-and-play solution, and it had to support best practices. They wanted a solution which fitted in with their 'digital first' approach; a cloud-based solution which meant they didn't have to heavily invest in IT infrastructure.

"Instead, we have put that money towards helping animals” - Helen Tracey.
