It doesn't take a sledgehammer to crack a nut

RKL, Sage and Stewart and Jasper Orchards relationship goes from strength to strength

{% include case-study-rspca-tabs-rkl.html active='solution' %}

Stewart & Jasper turned to RKL eSolutions to advise on the appropriate solution to meet their needs. Sage Business Cloud Enterprise Management offered the functionality and tools they required to address the challenges they had identified.

In addition to hauling, shelling and processing almonds, S&J produces gift baskets. Enterprise Management helped integrate their retail operations allowing their POS system to send data directly into the Sage ERP solution, helping to streamline business operations.

As a process manufacturer using Enterprise Management, S&J utilizes recipes to manage ingredient usage and production costs. Recipes are used to track the quantity requirements and costs of individual items, such as bulk almonds, flavorings, and sugar per batch and lot. Enterprise Management also gave S&J the ability to forecast ingredient requirements and production planning for each final product to meet its growing demand.

Using lot tracking functionality within Enterprise Management, S&J can now track the shelf life for each product and also be better prepared should there a product recall occur. Both combining to ensure a consistent customer experience.
