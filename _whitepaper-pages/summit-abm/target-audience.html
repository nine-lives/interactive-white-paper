do you have a clearly defined target audience and persona?

Establishing target accounts for an ABM campaign:

DO:
  • Choose accounts that represent ones you have easily sold to in the past
  • Make use of intent data (budget-allowing) to prioritise those prospects that are already open to purchase a product like yours
  • Include criteria such as tech stack if your solution holds up well against legacy products
DON'T:
  • Try to appeal to too many accounts – it will be hard to prioritise
  • Include accounts you’ve previously engaged with but had no success (particularly for ABM pilot programmes)
