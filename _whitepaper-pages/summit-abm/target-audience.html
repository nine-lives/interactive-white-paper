do you have a clearly defined target audience and persona?

Establishing target accounts for an ABM campaign:

DO:
  • Choose accounts which represent ones you have easily sold to previously
  • Make use of intent data (budget-allowing) to further filter your audience to prioritise those in market to purchase
  • Include criteria such as tech stack if your solution holds up well against legacy products
DON'T:
  • Have too wide an audience criteria – it will be hard to prioritise
  • Include accounts which you’ve previously had engagement with but no success for pilot programmes
