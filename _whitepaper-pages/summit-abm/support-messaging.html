Content Process

During the ABM Process we:

  • 1. Get to know your audience
  • 2. Define their specific frustrations and market pressures
  • 3. Familiarise ourselves with your value propositions of product and delivery
  • 4. Create the headline messages to bring to each account – including solutions to their specific problems and requirements
  • 5. Audit existing content, repurposing to support the new messaging where we can and creating new content where we need to
  • 6. Create the programme elements such as landing pages, copy, creatives – all bespoke to the target accounts or account groups
