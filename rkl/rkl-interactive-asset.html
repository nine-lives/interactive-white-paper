FOOD AND BEVERAGE MANUFACTURING IN TODAY’S WORLD

Today's Technological Business Challenges

Visibility through detailed, real-time data is key to competing.
Can you reduce risk by ensuring regulatory compliance?

Food and beverage organizations face more regulatory pressure every day and the Food Safety Modernization Act places new responsibilities on every food and beverage producer and distributor. You need traceability tools to allow you to handle expiration management, sustainability, allergen tracking, recalls and more in a streamlined and integrated way so you can demonstrate compliance while focussing on growing your business.

Do you have the insights you need to predict demand?

There is more uncertainty within the food and beverage industry than in almost any other. While you can’t predict some of these uncertainties such as crop failure and extreme weather, improved forecasting, real-time visibility and an integrated system can help you prepare and respond to market changes reducing inefficiencies and waste and ultimately save you money.

Can you manage a global network of partners and customers?

With a distribution center in one country, headquarters in another, and customers in a third, traditional means of communication aren’t enough to keep everyone connected. You need to be able to monitor and balance the interactions between these different entities, and that means having complete real-time visibility into how your business is operating.

Are your quality control and assurance processes fully integrated?

For food and beverage manufacturers, quality control is a never-ending process that is both time consuming and costly. Using legacy systems or a myriad of spreadsheets to track formulas, maintain batch information or monitor lots across the production change is inefficient. Your customers demand consistent quality or your reputation could be at stake. You need an integrated business system designed to support process manufacturing that can help you meet today’s quality control requirements.

Can you optimize continuously?

Managing and growing your business while keeping abreast of ever changing regulatory and compliance requirements is challenging. You need your data to support your manufacturing processes, automate compliance reporting and improve integration and efficiency across your organization. Do you have the insights to achieve this?

Are you employing technology to stay ahead?

This is no time to be letting legacy systems hold you back. You need to replace what isn't pushing your business forward with modern, enterprise management solutions that are easy to use and can support you through the challenges that have developed over the last decade and position you for the future.

Overview
Stewart & Jasper Orchards (S&J) has been a family run business for over 60 years. S&J has expanded over the years to become a leader in hulling, shelling, processing, exporting and marketing almonds through both wholesale and retail channels.

The Challenge
Stewart & Jasper were looking for ways to improve the efficiency of their business and streamline operations from managing ingredient usage and production costs to integrating their retail offering. They also needed a way to protect their brand by ensuring every customer enjoys the same quality from every purchase.

The Solution
Stewart & Jasper turned to RKL eSolutions to advise on the appropriate solution to meet their needs. Sage Business Cloud Enterprise Management offered the functionality and tools they required to address the challenges they had identified.

In addition to hulling, shelling and processing almonds, S&J produces gift baskets. Enterprise Management helped integrate their retail operations allowing their POS system to send data directly into the Sage ERP solution, helping to streamline business operations.

As a process manufacturer using Enterprise Management, S&J utilizes recipes to manage ingredient usage and production costs. Recipes are used to track the quantity requirements and costs of individual items, such as bulk almonds, flavorings, and sugar per batch and lot. Enterprise Management also gave S&J the ability to forecast ingredient requirements and production planning for each final product to meet its growing demand.

Using lot tracking functionality within Enterprise Management, S&J can now track the shelf life for each product and also be better prepared should a product recall occur. Both combining to ensure a consistent customer experience.
